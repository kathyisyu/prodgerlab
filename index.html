THE PRODGER LAB

HIV Research at
Western University, Canada.

  • Find Out More

Over 70% of the global burden of infection of HIV and
two-thirds of new HIV infections occur in sub-Saharan Africa.

Our goal is to design new methods for HIV prevention and cure, which could have a significant impact on the HIV epidemic in sub-Saharan Africa.

Why and how does circumcision reduce the risk of HIV transmission in heterosexual men? What are the factors that contribute to the persistence of HIV and the formation of the HIV latent viral reservoir?
